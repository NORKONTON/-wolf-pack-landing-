About the Game

Wolf Catches Eggs

Control your wolf with simple taps or keyboard to catch falling eggs. Each egg gives you points that convert to WPK tokens.

Earn WPK Tokens

Convert your game points to WPK tokens. Level up to increase your earning rate. Refer friends for bonus rewards.

Referral System

Get 25% of your friend's first deposit, 15% from their referrals, and 5% from third level. Build your pack!

TON Blockchain

Built on TON for fast, cheap transactions. Deposit TON, earn WPK tokens, withdraw anytime to your wallet.

Tokenomics

Total supply: 1,000,000,000 WPK tokens

Token Distribution

  • Game Rewards 400M (40%)
  • Project Treasury 300M (30%)
  • Marketing & Airdrops 150M (15%)
  • Team & Agent 100M (10%)
  • Liquidity (DEX) 50M (5%)

TON Treasury Management

Revenue from deposits and fees is allocated:

  • 50% → Liquidity pools (STON.fi/DeDust)
  • 30% → Development & Marketing
  • 10% → Reserve Fund
  • 10% → Token Buyback & Burn

Buyback mechanism: When WPK price drops, treasury buys tokens from market and burns them to support price.
